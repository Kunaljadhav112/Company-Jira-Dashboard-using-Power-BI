# 📊 Jira Campaign Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

A dynamic and interactive **Jira Campaign Performance Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to monitor, analyze, and optimize campaign operations across multiple business dimensions.

This dashboard provides leadership and marketing teams with real-time insights into campaign distribution, status tracking, regional performance, and vertical-wise segmentation — enabling faster and data-driven decision making.

Built a real-time Jira Dashboard in Power BI by integrating data directly from the Jira REST API, enabling automated reporting, campaign tracking and performance analysis for improved project visibility.

I am sharing a version that was previously used for testing and demonstration purposes (data is not updated). The production dashboard contains real-time and confidential company data, which is directly pulled into Power BI via the Jira REST API. As per company policy and data confidentiality guidelines, I am not authorized to share the fully updated or live production version.
---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The **Jira Campaign Dashboard** consolidates campaign-level data from Jira and transforms it into actionable insights through interactive visualizations.

It allows stakeholders to:

- Track total campaign volume
- Monitor campaign progress and bottlenecks
- Analyze vertical and regional distribution
- Identify opportunity losses
- Filter campaigns by SPOC, priority, status, and time period

---

## 📌 Key Features

### 1️⃣ Executive KPI Summary
- **Total Campaigns Counter**
- Snapshot view of overall campaign pipeline

### 2️⃣ Campaigns by Verticals
- Bar chart distribution of campaigns across:
  - FinTech
  - Education
  - BFSI
  - E-Commerce
  - Gaming
  - Entertainment
  - D2C
  - Travel & Tourism
  - Healthcare
  - Real Estate
- Quickly identifies dominant and underperforming verticals

### 3️⃣ Campaigns by Region
- Donut chart visualization of:
  - PAN India
  - Rest of World
  - Middle East & North Africa
  - India – North
  - India – South
- Helps assess geographic concentration

### 4️⃣ Campaign Status Monitoring
Horizontal bar chart tracking:
- Pending
- Opportunity Lost
- In Progress
- Campaign Live
- To Do

This helps identify pipeline health and operational delays.

### 5️⃣ Campaign Category Tracking (Board Status)
- In Progress
- Done
- To Do
- Visual share percentage for workflow monitoring

### 6️⃣ Interactive Filters
Users can dynamically filter data by:
- SPOC Name
- Campaign Vertical
- Campaign Priority
- Campaign Status
- Date Range (Month filter)
